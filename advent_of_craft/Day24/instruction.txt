Day 24: The celebration night.
Your last battle was a full victory, and you are ready to celebrate with your kingdom. A big celebration is being prepared.

Today is about indulgence, excess and feast.

On today's challenge, the last thing you want is to think about your craft and as such, the point of the exercise is to increase complexity of the code.

Yes, you heard right.

Using the Crappy-Driven Development technique, you have to make your code the crappiest possible.

"The secret art of making yourself indispensable by writing crappy code !!!"

Challenge of day 24: Write the most complicated code you can.
